# Data Dictionary

## Columns
- `id`: Unique identifier
- `name`: Name of the individual
- `age`: Age in years
- `score`: Test score (out of 100)

## Sample Data
| id | name     | age | score |
|----|----------|-----|-------|
| 1  | Jack Boesen | 22  | 88    |
| 2  | Ronnie McDoodle | 25  | 94    |
